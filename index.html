How it works

Simple process. You send clips + server info, we turn it into a promo that looks like it belongs on a front page.

Step 1

Drop your clips + info

Send your best moments (or raw footage) + what you want to promote: season launch, applications, event, new plugin, etc.

Step 2

We storyboard the vibe

We map the hook, pacing, text moments, and music direction so the promo feels clean — not random.

Step 3

Edit + polish

Color grade, motion, text, sound design, transitions — all built around your SMP theme.

Step 4

Deliver + revision

You get the final export(s) + one revision round to lock it in. Extra versions available if needed.
